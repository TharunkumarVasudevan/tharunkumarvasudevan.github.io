Experience

Bacterial DNA Extraction and Sample Preparation:

I performed bacterial DNA extraction from various microbial samples, ensuring a 95% success rate in DNA retrieval. This was achieved by optimizing the extraction protocol to maximize yield and purity. The DNA extracted was subsequently used in downstream molecular analyses such as PCR amplification and sequencing.

Antibiotic Sensitivity Testing:

I conducted antibiotic sensitivity assays on Escherichia coli and Bacillus subtilis, determining the minimum inhibitory concentration (MIC) for a range of antibiotics. This research provided critical insights into antimicrobial resistance patterns, contributing to the identification of effective treatments against these bacterial strains

ELISA for Antibody Detection:

I utilized the Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) to detect specific antibodies in biological samples, achieving an accuracy rate of 90%. The assay was optimized through spectrophotometric analysis, ensuring precise quantification of antibody levels, which is vital for diagnostic applications and biomarker detection.

Flow Cytometry for Cell Analysis:

I applied flow cytometry techniques to analyze cell populations and assess parameters such as cell viability, apoptosis, and surface marker expression. This tool allowed for high-throughput analysis of cell characteristics, providing insights into cell behavior and helping refine experimental conditions for further research.

Routine Lab Maintenance and Support:

I assisted in routine laboratory operations, including equipment sterilization, cleaning, and inventory management. I adhered to strict aseptic techniques to prevent contamination in microbial cultures, ensuring a controlled environment for ongoing experiments.

Collaboration and Teamwork:

I worked closely with lab members on multiple research projects, facilitating data collection, analysis, and task distribution. My collaborative approach ensured the smooth execution of experiments and maintained the overall efficiency of the laboratory.
